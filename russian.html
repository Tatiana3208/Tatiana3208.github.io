🇷🇺 Russian

📖 About Russian

Russian is an East Slavic language spoken by over 250 million people worldwide. It uses the Cyrillic alphabet and is known for its rich literature, complex grammar, and melodic intonation.

  • Alphabet: 33 letters (Cyrillic)
  • Grammar: 6 cases, verb aspects, movable stress
  • Native to: Russia, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan
